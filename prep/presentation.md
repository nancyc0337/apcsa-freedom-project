# Presentation Plan

## Hook

Question: Have you been disorganized and you got a lot of tasks on your mind? Do you feel sad lately and would like to feel happy? Well, you’re in luck!

My project is a multifunctional app that has 2 functions: a quotes generator, full of positive, motivational quotes and to-do list where it holds all your tasks and you can delete them when you are done.
